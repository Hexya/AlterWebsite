THE UNKNOW

As fascinating as 'scary' the unknown takes a huge place in our lives. Naturally the human fear new or unknown situations. Even if it is a mechanism of protection the fear of the unknown today can be a real brake for our personal fulfillment. Alter’s ambition is to make you more comfortable with this notion by proving how enriching it can be to go towards the unknown.
